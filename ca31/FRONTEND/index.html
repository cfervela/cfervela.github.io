Welcome to the Austen Library!

2025 marks the 250th anniversary of Jane Austen's birth. This library was opened in her honor in her birthplace of Hampshire.

This library has a curated collection of all of her works as well as some of the books that have been inspired by them.

This collection also includes some of the movie and series adaptations that have sprung from her wonderful works.
